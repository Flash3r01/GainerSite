Team Fortress 2

Team Fortress 2 is a class based shooter released in 2007. It has a multitude of different and unique gamemodes to choose from. For example: King of the Hill, Capture the Flag, Attack/Defend (Control Point), Symmetrical Control Point, Domination Control Point, Payload, Payload Race, Mann vs. Machine and many more.

There is also a inventory and loadout system which lets you find and equip new weapons. Some of them only have small stat changes while other completely change how you play the game. Of course there are also comsetics and especially HATS to customize each class. The community is still pretty active and there are also trading servers in which you can trade your unwanted items for something usefull.
