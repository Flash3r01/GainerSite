Terraria

Terraria is a 2D adventure game released in 2011. It started as a Minecraft clone with a focus on combat. Since then it has received a lot of big content additions that fundamentally changed the feeling of the game. Its gameplay can be split in two phases:

In the first phase the game focuses on exploring the world and defending yourself against common enemies, giant eyeballs and even invasions.

In the second phase the player is much more active, inviting otherworldly monsters to their world just to slay them and collect their riches.
